Rajat Bhattacharjya

rajatb1 [at] uci [dot] edu

Hi! I am a PhD student at the University of California, Irvine in the Department of Computer Science. At UCI, I'm a part of the Dutt Research Group advised by Distinguished Prof. Nikil Dutt. Previously, I worked as a Researcher at TCS Research in the Computing Systems Lab. Before that, I was a Software Engineer at Parallel Wireless, Inc., where I worked on 5G NR and 2G access stack development for 1.5 years.

I completed my undergraduate studies in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati in May 2021. During my undergraduate studies, I have worked on approximate computing and its various applications.

In my undergraduate journey, I was advised by Dr. Dip Sankar Banerjee, Dr. Soumyajit Poddar, Dr. Neeraj Goel, and Prof. Muhammad Shafique.

My research interests include approximate computing, embedded systems, signal processing, autonomous systems, and VLSI Design. I try investigating and working on interesting research problems in my free time. In case you want to know more about my research or want to collaborate with me, please feel free to send me an email.
